Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

This is a great kimchi fried rice recipe that can be tailored to your own tastes

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Cold rice
  • Seasame oil
  • Protein of choice

Steps

  1. Stir fry kimchi for 1 min.
  2. Cook protein
  3. Add rice and leftover kimchi juice
  4. Add seasame oil
