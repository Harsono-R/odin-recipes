Pasta

Description

This recipe makes 5 delicious services of bolognese pasta. Perfect for meal prepping or parties!

Ingredients

  • Dry pasta of choice
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Water
  • Onion
  • Ground meat of choice

Steps

  1. Boil water & add salt
  2. Add pasta when water is boiling
  3. Cook for 11-12 mins.
  4. Saute onions and brown meat
  5. Add pasta sauce
  6. Serve pasta with generous amounts of sauce
